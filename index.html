Ludum Dare

During the weekend I will be participating in the game jam Ludum Dare. It's one of the longest running and overall biggest game Jams. The goal is to create the best game in 72 hours. I am super excited!
Hopefully I will be able to push something out, maybe even something worth playing but lets not get our hopes up to high. For the competition I will use the unity engine, coding in c#. I might use blender if I decide to go 3D (which I'm trying to convince my self not to, I haven't got the time, but its so much more fun). I'll use Visual Studio as my IDE and Inkscape for UI/sprites, LMMS & Audacity for audio.
